With the immense grace of Allah, the blessings of righteous individuals, the approval of the Muhyiddin Aandavar Jummah Masjid administration, and the guidance of its esteemed Imam, this volunteer group was initiated by dedicated Islamic youth from our village. Alhamdulillah.

üéØ Objective of the Volunteer Group

Witnessing the struggles of some families in extreme poverty, who even find it difficult to afford daily meals, the group was started with the noble intention of providing monthly food assistance to those in need.

üë®‚Äçüë©‚Äçüëß Initial Assistance Efforts

Although many people face financial hardship, our initial focus was on providing support to households where women are struggling without any source of income due to the absence of male earners.

ü§ù Volunteer-Based Collection & Growth

Without any compulsion, this initiative is run solely through the voluntary contributions of individuals who willingly support the cause. The group started in March 2016 with the contributions of 14 donors. Now, with the continued generosity of 54 donors, the initiative is running successfully.

Alhamdulillah.

üè° Assistance to Families

Initially, we supported 10 families, and gradually, the number was increased. Currently, we are providing assistance to 47 families.

üóì 10 Years of Service

Since its inception in March 2016, this initiative has continued without any interruptions, including during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Alhamdulillah.
